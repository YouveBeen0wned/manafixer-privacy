Privacy Policy — Fixed

Effective date: April 20, 2026  |  Last updated: April 20, 2026

Fixed ("the App") is a Magic: The Gathering deck-scanning and manabase calculator app for Android, developed by CCM Inspired. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Camera data. The App requests access to your device camera solely to photograph Magic: The Gathering card names for on-device text recognition. Camera images are processed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit and are never transmitted to our servers or stored permanently.

Card lookup data. When a card name is identified, the App sends that name to the Scryfall API (scryfall.com) to retrieve card details such as mana cost and card type. Scryfall's own Privacy Policy governs their handling of API requests.

Advertising data. The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve and measure advertisements. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.

Analytics. The App does not use any first-party analytics or crash reporting services. Basic, aggregated crash and stability data may be collected automatically by Google Play Services.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not store deck lists, scan history, or any user-generated content on external servers. All deck data exists only in device memory for the duration of your session.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Scryfall API — card data lookup. Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit — on-device text recognition. Terms
  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can delete it.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store user data on our servers, there is nothing to retain or delete on our end. Deck data stored in device memory is cleared when the App is closed.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we collect no personal data, these rights are largely not applicable. For advertising-related data, you can manage your preferences through Google's ad settings or your device's privacy settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
